PEPPER - A friendly contact widget for your website.

== Description ==

Does your website provide an easy and immediately visible way for visitors to get in touch; with YOU?

With PEPPER, your website's visitors won't ever have to CTRL-F again to find that odd "contact" link! PEPPER collects all your contact info in a pretty widget â€“ easy to find, yet unobtrusive!

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `pepper` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to `Settings` -> `Pepper` to customize the widget. Click `Safe`
4. Done - the widget is automatically displayed on your website!

== Frequently asked questions ==

**Which platforms are supported in the widget?**

For starters, we've added all the basics: Facebook, Twitter, Messenger, Snapchat and many more. Even good ol' email and phone! ;) Missing a platform? Shoot us an email at pepper@swat.io!

**Which languages are supported in the widget?**

Right now we offer English, German, Chinese (traditional), Czech, Spanish, French, Finnish, Dutch, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Slovenian, Swedish, Turkish. If you want to help out with more translations, get in touch at pepper@swat.io!
